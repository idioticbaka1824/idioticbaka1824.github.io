Music

In 2004, independent developer Daisuke "Pixel" Amaya released a free game called Cave Story. He also made a custom music-making software specifically for making the music in this game, called Organya Maker, or 'Org Maker' for short. This 16-bit software, albeit somewhat limited, is very easy to learn and use. While I have no experience or knowledge of music theory, I have attempted to make some 'sound doodles' with this software. You can listen to them here.
You can also play .org files from external sources by inputting the direct URL, if so desired.

Organya-JS, which I am using to play .org files in-browser, is courtesy of Alula.
